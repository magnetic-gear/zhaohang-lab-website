ZHANG Yujia received the B.Eng. degree in mechanical engineering from South China University of Technology, Guangzhou, China, in 2023. He is currently pursuing a M.Phil. in Robotics and Autonomous Systems at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), Guangzhou. His research interests include control of multi-phase machines, optimization techniques, online neural network techniques and signal processing.
